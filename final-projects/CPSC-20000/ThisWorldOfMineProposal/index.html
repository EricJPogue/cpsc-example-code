<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Application Overview</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">My application will allow the end user to checkoff the places that they have traveled to. It will be their own checklist of the countries they have visited. Additionally, they can write a journal entry of what they liked and disliked about each place.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Technology Overview</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">My application with be written with HTML and will be hosted through Azure.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Proposal Last Updated</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">Thursday December 16, 2021</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Feature List</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">A list of all countries where the end user may place a checkmark next to each country that they have traveled to. There will be a section where the end user can put in the countries they plan to visit soon as well as a section where one can explain their thoughts on the places they have visited.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Future Feature List</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">A world map where each country would light up when the end user would hover their mouse over it, showing what country it is. Additionally, when clicked, the webpage sends you to Expedia to book a flight.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Basic Technical Features</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">I will use previous knowledge that I have on HTML pages to make my application have a nice layout. I will try and make the page very appealing to the eye, and maybe even add some cool features that I have yet to learn.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Advanced Technical Features</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">I would like to try and make the page as unique as it can be. Unsure of how I would do this, but it would look a lot more detailed than this basic overview. A nice world map would look really cool.</p>

<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Original Ideas</h2>
<p style="font-family:courier;">I was going to have a simple user interface where the user was able to have a list where they can write down where they want to travel to and then check them off as they go. Additionally, they can write journal entries for it.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">Final Ideas</h2>
<p style="font-family:courier;">I ended up finding a cool globe that showed the world map and allowed the user to hover over each country to see what country it is, and when clicked on, the page would take you to Expedia, to book a flight. There ended up being a checklist that allowed the user to write their dream destinations, take them off, and even cross/uncross them off. Additionally, at the bottom, one could recieve a final copy of their input on the Web Page, and then also get emails sent to them about travel offers.</p>
<h3 style="font-family:courier;">If I Had More Time...</h3>
<p style="font-family:courier;">I most likely would have tried to have that whenever someone enter a country in the checklist, it would change colors on the globe to show that that is where they have traveled to. Additionally, I would even go a step further and when one clicked on it, it gave a link for a flight to go there right now, intead of the overall travel link of Expedia. Finally, I would make the button on the bottom work so others could recieve information on travel destinations and get a copy of their results.</p>
