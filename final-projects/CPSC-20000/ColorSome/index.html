ColorSome!

Welcome to ColorSome! In this minigame, you'll simply be tasked to color in the bear below to however you would like! Let your creativity guide you here, there are no limits to how you color it in. Grizzly Bear? Easy. Care Bear? Go for it. Polar Bear? Why not? Below the bear is a color picker which you'll be able to use to color your very own bear in, the sky's the limit!
